Charters: Fishing for Everyone.

A charter boat carrying you or your group only. A private fishing charter is when everyone on the boat is from the same group for one fee. These trips are not priced as a per person rate. Only you and your family or friends are on the boat. You are paying for the entire boat to take you fishing.

Our Charter Prices

  • 2-4 Hours: $399 for up to 10 people
  • 4-6 Hours: $599 for up to 10 people
  • 6-8 Hours: $799 for up to 10 people
  • 10 Hours+: $1099 for up to 10 people (Max 12 Hours)
